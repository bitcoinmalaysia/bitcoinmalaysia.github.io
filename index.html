What is BTCM?

Bitcoin Malaysia (BTCM) is a next-generation decentralized digital currency built on the BNB Chain, inspired by the foundational vision of Bitcoin but tailored for the future of Malaysia. BTCM was created by Azlan Nakamoto, a visionary founder who believes Malaysia deserves a sovereign, community-driven asset to lead Southeast Asia into the blockchain era.

BTCM aims to go beyond just being a cryptocurrency — it is a symbol of national innovation, digital empowerment, and financial independence for all Malaysians. Designed with a fixed supply of 21 million tokens, BTCM reflects scarcity, value, and long-term growth potential — similar to Bitcoin, but made by and for the people of Malaysia.

Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a crypto investor, or a proud Malaysian, BTCM invites you to be part of a new economic movement where blockchain meets national pride. Our mission is to build a lasting digital legacy that uplifts local innovation and puts Malaysia on the global crypto map — quietly, confidently, and with purpose.
