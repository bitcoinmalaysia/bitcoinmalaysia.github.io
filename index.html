About BTCM

Bitcoin Malaysia (BTCM) is a rare digital asset created to represent the spirit of decentralization and self-sovereignty within Malaysia. It is not a meme coin — it is a serious, symbolic currency of the people, with a total supply of only 21 million. BTCM was launched quietly, without hype, and built to grow steadily through community strength, transparency, and strong leadership by its founder, Azlan Nakamoto.

About BTCA

BTCA (Bitcoin Asean) is the regional expansion of BTCM — a token designed to unite blockchain believers across Southeast Asia. With a total supply of 42 million, it honors the 10 ASEAN countries while doubling Bitcoin’s famous cap. Though still early in its development, BTCA is expected to support regional projects and economic innovation in the Web3 space.
